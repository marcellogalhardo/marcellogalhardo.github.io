About

Software engineer with over 10 years of experience. I have been building software professionally since 2012, starting on Web and Backend, later on Desktop, and finally, on Android. I worked with small teams at Quandoo, to dozens of engineers working on the same codebase at N26 and Delivery Hero. At N26, I worked on the Consumer App and later led the Android Platform Team. The apps I worked on had millions of downloads used in more than 20 countries.

Currently, I work in the AndroidX Team at Google in London, bringing Kotlin multi-platform support to some of the most used Android Libraries.

I am a moderator of the Android Dev BR community, the largest network of Android developers in Brazil.
